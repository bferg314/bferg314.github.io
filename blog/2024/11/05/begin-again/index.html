    Begin Again

    I have been trying to gather my things together. It has been a mess of random notes, docs, published things here and there over the years. And attempts at stuff like this.

    I have actually been writing Morning Pages almost daily since October 2024, but I believe this will serve a different purpose. And there was a plugin for it, so I guess that's part of the appeal. I'm a sucker for a plugin.

    So I'll se what I can do to get some stuff in here. I certainly think I can place my guides and articles here, and maybe even some random story nonsense I have sitting around from over the years. What's the worst that will happen? People will see it and have opinions? We all do that every day all the time and ever so promptly forget.

    I really enjoy Sam Harris and his Waking Up app. In many of the morning meditations he will ask you in the last minute to take a moment, forget everything you have been doing before this (since you probably got lost in a thought) and begin again. So that is what this is. Yet another "Begin Again."
